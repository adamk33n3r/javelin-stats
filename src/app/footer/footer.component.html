Contact

Send bug reports or feedback to admin@javelin-stats.com

I can also be reached at /u/kyngston on reddit or Kyngston#4859 on Discord.

If you are submitting a bug for incorrect damage calculations, please include a link to your build, and a photo or video of the damage number shown on screen. Be sure that the damage numbers are non-weak-point hits on red-health-bar mobs. Thanks!
